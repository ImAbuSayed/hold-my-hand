[Hook]
Pull a little closer, leave the world outside
Let the shadows fall, we have nothing left to hide
I can feel the rhythm, the beating of your heart
Hold my hand right now, and let the magic start

[Verse 1]
The coffee on the table, the candles burning low
There is nowhere else but here, nowhere else to go
Your fingers trace the edges, inching next to mine
I want to drink this moment, sweeter than the wine

The distance is evaporating, disappearing fast
I want to make this single second forever last

[Chorus]

We can take it slow, there is so much left to learn

Let the tender silence ignite a blazing spark
